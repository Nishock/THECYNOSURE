Distinguisher

Nowaday's road accidents is major problem all over the world . As report by WHO in its first global status report 80,000 of Indian people are killed on roads due to over speeding, drunk driving and drowsiness. The driver lost his control when he/she is about to sleep . The project can generate a model that can prevent such accidents. In this model the eye blink sensor is placed near the eyes on goggle to sense the blink time of eyes. Highways accidents are very prevalent and our design would be help to this problem by monitoring the eyelid movement of driver . Using a sensor to compare an open eye to a closed eye for a prolonged period will be the basis of our project .
