Human following robot

Science porium

In the rapid moving world, now there is a need of robot such as"Human Following Robot" that can interact and co-exit with them. In this robot we have use arduino uno, DC motors, ultrasonic sensor, IR sensor and for giving command we use C/C++ programming languages. Our system consist of four wheel robotic vehicle mounted with a separate microprocessor and control unit along with different sensor which help them to move. IR sensor controls the movement of sensor detect the obstacles and stops the motor. And their is a need of such robot that can assist humans and can serve them.

Smart City

Visioneries

Our project is The City From The Future. Our model comprises of Windmills, Modern Buildings, Greenery and a factory. So, our concept is to change solar energy into electrical energy... Charging the whole city using solar panels. The windmills are there to convert the wind energy into electrical energy. The factory we've is for taking care of the fire emergency take place in the city, our factory immediately came into action if any emergency took place. And we've taken care of the environment as well cause the most important thing that makes a city smart is it's clean and green environment. This is our beautiful City From The Future/ The Smart City.
